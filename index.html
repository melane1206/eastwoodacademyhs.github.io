Lunch Bunch

The Lunch Bunch Club's purpose is to unite the school community and foster friendships! Over time, Lunch Bunch has grown from 8 members to 25% of the school population, making us one of the biggest clubs at Eastwood Academy. The Lunch Bunch continues to promote unity between people and the East End community. We unite the school by fundraisers, events, and socials. Our biggest project was the spring festival in 2024, which was open to all. It allowed us to unite students, teachers, and the promotion of small businesses. The three values of The lunch Bunch are food, fun, and friendship. To find more visit our Instagram @eahs.lunchbunch.

IV Nurses Club

Welcome to the IV Nurses! In this club we strive to educate you as much as we can on different types of nursing. Our meetings take place during Monday club advocacy. Sometimes we bring in guest speakers and often times we have research projects that include presentations or posters to put around our school! With the IV Nurses you can be better prepared and equipped with knowledge that can help or give you a head start in nursing school. Girls Soccer Club

The EAHS Girls Soccer Team has been around for more than nearly 20 years, even before Coach Salgado was a student here himself. After taking over the team from his former teachers - Mr. Perez and Mr. Duron - Coach Salgado has reinvented the team from the ground up to become more about "girl power". The girls soccer team is not just about playing soccer, but also about a group of young women to support and empower each other through a physically demanding and community-building sport. We combine hard, physical conditioning, and effective communication along with expressing our femininity in a sport that has been heavily male-centered for decades. Together, we celebrate our wins, stick together through losses, uplift one another after injuries, and improve as a team and as individuals in the mind, body, and soul - all while having fun on and off the practice and playing on the field. We love all the support from our friends, families, and community, and we welcome all girls of any skill level to join our family of young eager women looking to find a way to stay fit, have fun, and make friends. For more information, check us out at @eahs_wfc on Instagram!

Civic Engagement

The civic Engagement Club was established in 2024. In this club, we prioritize and engage in learning more about voting and our rights. We allow students to flourish, do research relevant to our community's problems, and propose solutions. For more insight into what we do, follow us on Instagram @civicengagement_eahs.

Architect Club

It was during the club fair that Ivan and I started the Architecture Club. Although it was created on the spur of the moment, we truly made it our goal to provide interested students a taste of what college architecture students would study and construct. We have traveled to a university to view the campus's structures and architectural program. Additionally, we have given the students access to summer programs and competitions. Being an architect, Mr. Mechler gives us the chance to learn about his personal experiences as well as the advantages and disadvantages, which is a great opportunity for those who are interested. As the year progresses, Ivan and I hope to help them begin building models, assist them in entering architecture programs, and take them on more field trips to discover the many architectural styles.

Gardening Club

The Roots of Eastwood focus on maintaining the health of the school's gardens and educating its members on growing their own vegetation. The club meets two days a week, often focusing on planting new seeds, moving plants if necessary, and altering the new pathway we made for the students. These activities allow for members to understand how to care for plants and monitor plants' progression in different stages. Additionally, the members have grown to become more comfortable using tools like spades, shears, rakes, etc. The club doesn't particularly focus on earning money but instead collecting resources like seeds to help any flourishment.

National Honors Society

The National Honor Society is a nationally recognized organization. Members of NHS are those who excel in academic virtue and portray scholarly excellence. The pillars of NHS that each member must uphold are scholarship, service, leadership, and character. The National Honors Society provides various opportunities to recieve community service hours.
